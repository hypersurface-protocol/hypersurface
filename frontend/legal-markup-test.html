Action Authorization Token Agreement

This document serves as an Action Authorization Token Agreement ("Agreement"), put forth by the Issuer, conferring upon the Recipient the authorization to perform a specified action. This token is redeemable for the execution of a share transfer through the submission of this form to the URL specified in the form's action attribute.

1. Grant of Authority

The Issuer grants the Recipient the authority to execute a share transfer of Series A Common shares of Company, as specified in this redeemable Action Authorization Token. The transfer is facilitated through a call to the specified URL, under the predefined conditions of this Agreement.
